Are you searching for comprehensive and compassionate mental health care in Bhubaneswar? Dr. Jyosil Kumar Bhol stands out as a highly qualified, RCI-licensed Clinical Psychologist, renowned for his commitment to excellence in psychological assessment, therapy, and research.

About Dr. Jyosil Kumar Bhol

Dr. Bhol currently serves as Consultant Clinical Psychologist at Ankura Hospital, Bhubaneswar. With an impressive academic track record, he holds an M.Phil. in Clinical Psychology from the esteemed Mental Health Institute (CoE), SCB Medical College and Hospital, Cuttack, and is pursuing a Ph.D. in Psychology at Kalinga Institute of Industrial Technology (KIIT), Bhubaneswar. Earlier, he earned his Master's degree in Clinical Psychology from Utkal University, graduating as a rank holder—a testament to his dedication and expertise.

Diverse Experience in Mental Health Services

With over four years of hands-on experience, Dr. Bhol has worked with leading institutions, including the State Institute for Empowerment of Persons with Disabilities (SIEP), Kalinga Institute of Medical Sciences (KIMS), and Maa Neuropsychiatric Clinic. His background equips him to address complex psychological concerns across varied populations—children, adolescents, and adults.

Areas of Expertise

  • Detailed Clinical Assessments: Comprehensive history taking and diagnostic clarification.
  • Psychometric & Neuropsychological Testing: IQ assessments, personality evaluations, projective tests such as the Rorschach, and more.
  • Therapeutic Interventions: Behavioral therapies, cognitive therapies, and counseling tailored to individual needs.
  • Specialized Support: Management of substance use disorders, marital and relationship issues, academic stress, developmental and emotional challenges.

Research & Academic Contributions

Staying at the forefront of mental health research, Dr. Bhol has published works on substance abuse and child psychiatry, with his broader research interests including emotional intelligence, motivation in learning, and the psychosocial impacts of mental illness. He is a regular presenter at state-level psychiatric conferences and workshops, sharing insights that help advance psychological care.

Client-Centered, Evidence-Based Care

Dr. Bhol integrates holistic, evidence-based approaches with empathy and understanding, ensuring every client receives tailored psychological support. His mission is to help individuals build resilience, enhance emotional well-being, and lead more fulfilling lives.
